Our store hours are designed to accommodate your dining needs throughout the week. We are open from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM from Monday to Saturday, providing ample time for you to enjoy our authentic African cuisine. On Sundays, we operate from 12:00 PM to 8:00 PM, allowing you to savor our dishes during a more relaxed weekend schedule. Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, a leisurely dinner, or a weekend treat, The African Bowl is here to serve you with delicious meals and warm hospitality.
